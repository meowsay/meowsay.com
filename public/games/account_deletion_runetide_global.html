Account & Data Deletion

Game: RuneTide: Idle Fishing RPG
Last updated: May 1, 2026
Contact: fanfanshao@gmail.com
Important: RuneTide: Idle Fishing RPG does not provide a developer-hosted account system or cloud save service. We do not store your game account, character progress, or personal profile data on our own servers.

How to Delete Your Data

Because the game does not maintain a server-side account, there is no online account for us to close. All gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device, or may be associated with platform services provided by Google Play or Apple.

To delete your local game data, use one of the following methods:

  1. Uninstall RuneTide: Idle Fishing RPG from your device.
  2. Optionally clear the app's local storage from your device settings before uninstalling, if your device offers that option.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Locally stored game progress
  • Local settings and preferences
  • Locally cached game resources created by the app

What We Do Not Store

  • No developer-hosted game account
  • No developer-hosted cloud save data
  • No developer-hosted personal profile database for this game

Support Requests and Contact Deletion

If you previously contacted us by email and want us to delete that support correspondence, you may send a request to fanfanshao@gmail.com with the subject line RuneTide Data Deletion Request.

In your request, please include:

  • The email address you used to contact us
  • The game name: RuneTide: Idle Fishing RPG
  • A short description of the data you want removed

We will review and process eligible deletion requests for support emails within a reasonable time, subject to any legal or security obligations.

Third-Party Platform Data

Some data related to achievements, purchases, cloud backups, advertising, or platform services may be managed by Google Play, Apple, or other third-party providers. If you want those records removed, you may need to manage them directly through your platform account or the relevant provider.
